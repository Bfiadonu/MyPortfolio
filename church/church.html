First Love North America

Best place to be on a Sunday

First Love church was founded by Bishop Dag Heward Mills in the year 2012. Every Sunday, thousands of young people meet in different first love locations all over the world to meet God. In the year 2018 Rev Orleans Lindsay pioneered the First love movement in North America. After a year and a half he handed over to Bishop Henry Asare who is currently the head of First Love in North America. The best place to be on a Sunday is First love church, where lives are changed and destinies are restored! #ComeAndSee
